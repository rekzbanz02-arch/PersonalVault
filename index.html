Welcome to PersonaVault 🔐

PersonaVault is your all-in-one personal records management system. All data is securely stored in your own Google Sheets spreadsheet, giving you full ownership and control over your information.

Getting Started:

  • Log in using your passcode
  • Navigate modules using the sidebar
  • Create groups to organize records
  • Add entries within each group
  • Use the global search (Ctrl+K) to find anything

Modules Guide 📁

PersonaVault contains 9 record modules:

  • 👤 Personal Details — Store info about yourself, family, and contacts. Age is auto-calculated from birthdate.
  • 🎓 Education — Track formal education, certificates, and skills.
  • 💼 Work History — Employment records with automatic tenure calculation.
  • 🏥 Medical Records — Diagnoses, medications, vaccinations, and lab results.
  • 💰 Financial — Full income/expense tracking with charts and totals.
  • 🔑 Password Vault — Encrypted credential storage. Require passcode to reveal.
  • 📅 Events — Events and milestones with countdowns.
  • ⏰ Schedules — Alarms and reminders with browser notifications.
  • 📝 Journal — Personal notes and diary entries.

Sync & Storage ☁️

All data is stored in your Google Sheets spreadsheet via Apps Script. Each module uses its own sheet tab.

The sync indicator in the header shows your connection status: 🟢 Online, 🟡 Syncing, ⚫ Offline.

Data is cached locally in sessionStorage to reduce API calls. Use the Sync button to force a refresh.

Security 🔒

PersonaVault uses a passcode to protect access. The passcode is stored as a hashed value in localStorage.

Passwords in the Password Vault are stored obfuscated and require re-entering your passcode to reveal.

Sensitive data (passwords) is automatically excluded from all exports.

FAQ ❓

Q: How do I set up Google Sheets integration?
A: Go to Settings → Connection. Paste your Apps Script Web App URL and API Key from your Google Apps Script deployment.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Your data lives in your own Google Sheets, not on any third-party server. The passcode protects the app.

Q: How do I backup my data?
A: Go to Settings → Data Management → Export All Data to download a full JSON backup.

Q: What if I forget my passcode?
A: Open browser devtools, go to Application → localStorage, and delete the pv_passcode key. You'll be prompted to reset.
